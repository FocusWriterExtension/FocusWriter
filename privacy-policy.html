Privacy Policy

At Focus Writer, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Focus Writer collects minimal information to provide its core functionality:

  • Word count goals you set
  • Writing progress statistics
  • Writing session duration

All of this information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Track your writing progress
  • Provide progress notifications
  • Save your writing goals
  • Generate writing statistics to help you stay motivated

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not maintain any external servers or databases for storing your information.

Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, or transfer your information to any third parties. Your writing data remains private and under your control.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No external transmission of your writing data
  • Regular security updates to the extension

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through the extension's interface
  • Delete your data at any time through the extension's settings
  • Disable or uninstall the extension, which will remove all stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly.
